![](file:////Users/baiyan/Library/Containers/com.kingsoft.wpsoffice.mac/Data/tmp/wps-baiyan/ksohtml//wps30.jpg) 

![](file:////Users/baiyan/Library/Containers/com.kingsoft.wpsoffice.mac/Data/tmp/wps-baiyan/ksohtml//wps31.jpg) 

点击【编辑课程公告】可再次检查课程基础信息

![](file:////Users/baiyan/Library/Containers/com.kingsoft.wpsoffice.mac/Data/tmp/wps-baiyan/ksohtml//wps32.jpg)
